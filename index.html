Gothic Architecture Features

Explore the key characteristics of Gothic architecture

▲ Pointed Arches Distinctive pointed arch shapes direct structural weight and create vertical emphasis.
♲ Ribbed Vaults Intersecting ribbed arches support the ceiling and enable complex, ornate designs.
🚪 Flying Buttresses External supports transfer roof weight, allowing for taller, thinner walls and vast windows.
📷 Stained Glass Windows Large, colorful windows fill interiors with vibrant light, often arranged in intricate patterns.
🏠 Vertical Emphasis Soaring towers, spires, and tall ceilings create a sense of height and grandeur.
👹 Ornamentation Intricate stonework, sculpted figures, and decorative facades are hallmarks of Gothic style.
